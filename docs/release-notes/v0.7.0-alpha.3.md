# Fantasy Earth on Minecraft 0.7.0-alpha.3

Development test build for Minecraft 26.2 / NeoForge 26.2.0.7-beta.

- Keeps the Field Arcane Notebook as a permanent guide instead of consuming it for initiation.
- Makes research notes unlock circuits only; attunement resolves in the arcane panel or on a focus mat.
- Adds independent level-1-to-10 XP tracks and permanent rewards for aether, body ward and breathing circuits.
- Adds mana-costed active spells with a configurable key and independent cooldown.
- Adds real attack-progress and sitting-state animation inputs for familiars, the Runic Watcher and Crystal Armored Spider.
- Keeps Earth on Minecraft, Earth Human and Xuanhuan Earth integrations optional.
